About the Application

Pahimakas is a mobile application designed to streamline and enhance funeral service management. This app provides real-time support for families and funeral service providers, simplifying the scheduling of services, tracking logistics, and managing necessary arrangements.

  • Real-Time Notifications: Updates on funeral arrangements, changes, and service reminders.
  • Payment Options: Easy integration with payment gateways for smooth transactions.
  • Customizable Features: Language settings and notification preferences to cater to different user needs.
